Computers are an integral part of modern day life. As technological advancement continues, computers will become an even more integral part of the functioning of society. Information technology has always interested me, and that interest has motivated me to pursue a career in the field of information technology/software engineering with the goal of eventually working in the field of information security.

I am currently a college senior working towards getting a degree in Computer Science with a concentration in information security. As a student I have completed research through Kent State's Summer Undergraduate Research Experience internship as well as through the role of Undergraduate Student Research Assistant.

I have actively participated in several organizations and activities including HacKSU, The National Society of Leadership and Success Kent Chapter, Civil Air Patrol, and Cyberpatriot as well as leading the Kent State Stark campus Computer Club as president.

I have participated in multiple hackathons, advancing my knowledge and experience in software development, as well as leadership and teamwork skills, in an environment that is outside of the classroom.

Currently, I am attempting to get a summer internship/co-op with the goal of full time employment.
